# Week 1 — Relational Foundations

A catalog needs worlds to list. Create your first table — the planets.

## Concepts
- A **table** models one kind of thing; a **row** is one instance.
- A **column** has a **data type**; a **primary key** identifies each row; a
  **constraint** (`not null`, `check`) enforces a rule.

## Problems (in `assignments/week1/starter.sql`)
1. Create the `planets` table (name, type, radius_km, distance_au, mass_e24,
   mean_temp_c) with `CHECK` constraints.
2. Insert at least 6 planets.
3. Write a SELECT listing planets ordered by distance.

## Done when
- Planet cards appear in the catalog on the homepage.
- The Week 1 planet is **Unlocked**.
