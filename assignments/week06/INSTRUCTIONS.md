# Week 6 — SQL Fundamentals: SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE

Log telescope observations by creating an `observations` table and exercising all
four DML verbs on it. (You only touch your own new table.)

## Problems (in `assignments/week06/starter.sql`)
1. Create the `observations` table (foreign keys to astronomers and planets).
2. **INSERT** at least three observations.
3. **UPDATE** logged observations to confirmed, then **DELETE** a cancelled one.
4. **SELECT** the remaining observations.

## Done when
- An observation log appears once Week 10's join view is built.
- The Week 6 planet is **Unlocked**.
